What is OKR?


OKR stand for Objectives and Key Results, a collaborative goal-setting methodology used by teams and

individuals to set challenging, ambitious goals with measurable results. OKRs are how you track progress,

create alignment, and encourage engagement around measurable goals.


OBJECTIVE:

The Objective is qualitative and reminds you of what you set to improve.


KEY RESULT:

The Key Results are quantitative, measurable outcomes you need to deliver to help achieve the Objective.


EXAMPLE:

Objective:

Become a confident public speaker.

Key Results:

KR 1: Gain knowledge about public speaking within 2 weeks.

KR 2: Obtain knowledge in interested topics within a month.

KR 3: Gain 3000 followers online within 6 months.

KR 4: Participate in public speaking events atleast 3 times per month.
